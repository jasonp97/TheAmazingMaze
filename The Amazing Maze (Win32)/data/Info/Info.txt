* HOW TO PLAY:
  Move the player (white point) around the maze using arrow keys.
  Collect stars to earn points.
  Avoid touching enemies (blue dots).
  You have 3 lives.
  You will be brought to the respawn point each time you die.
  The green point is the destination.
  Help the player reaching the destination to win the game.
